Registration

Who can participate

The CSV 2026 Challenge is open to researchers, students and industrial teams interested in carotid plaque segmentation and vulnerability assessment in ultrasound. Both single-person and multi-person teams are welcome.

Important dates

  • Registration opens: July 1, 2025
  • Registration deadline: December 15, 2025
  • Final submission deadline: January 31, 2026

How to register

  1. Fill in the online registration form (one per team).
  2. Use an institutional e-mail address for the team contact person.
  3. After your registration is approved, you will receive credentials to access the dataset download portal and submission system.

Please use the following link to log in to the submission system: Entrance

If you need to update your team information after registration, please contact the organizers via the e-mail address listed on the Contact Us page.
